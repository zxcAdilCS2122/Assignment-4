This site will introduce you to the game of Dota 2

Dota 2 is a computer multiplayer team game in the genre of multiplayer online battle arena, developed by Valve

In this game, heroes are divided into three types:

  • Strength
  • Dexterity
  • Intelligence

Basic information about this game:

  • The game was announced on October 13, 2010.
  • There are currently 121 playable heroes in the game.
  • On average, more than 412 thousand people play Dota a day
