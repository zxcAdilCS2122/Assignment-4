List of heroes

On this page you can get acquainted with each hero
Dexterity
Agility is one of the three main attributes of the hero, increasing his armor and attack speed. Agility heroes also gain additional attack damage per point of Agility.
Strength

Strength is one of the three main attributes of the hero, increasing the amount of his health and the speed of recovery of health. Strength heroes also receive additional attack damage for each point of strength.
Intelligence

Intelligence is one of the three main attributes of a hero, increasing his mana and mana regeneration rate. Intellect heroes also gain additional attack damage per point of intelligence.
